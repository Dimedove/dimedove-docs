Find answers to the most common questions about Dimedove.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Dimedove?">
    Dimedove is a no-code agent building platform for businesses selling in the AI era. It helps qualify prospects and customers through intelligent conversations.
  </Accordion>

{" "}

<Accordion title="How do I install the agent on my website?">
  Simply add the JavaScript widget code to your website using your agent ID and
  publishable key. See our [Installation Guide](../platform/agents/install) for
  details.
</Accordion>

{" "}

<Accordion title="What are contact insights and attributes?">
  Contact insights are unstructured data points collected from conversations,
  while attributes are structured data points that make up contact cards for
  unified data across your contacts.
</Accordion>

{" "}

<Accordion title="How do webhooks work with Dimedove?">
  Webhooks send real-time notifications when events occur (chat started/ended,
  contact insights created, tasks completed). They include encrypted signatures
  for security verification.
</Accordion>

{" "}

<Accordion title="Can I customize my agent's behavior and personality?">
  Yes, you can configure your agent's identity, instructions, personality, tone,
  first message, and many other behavioral settings to match your business
  needs.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What integrations are available?">
  We're building integrations with HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, Attio,
  Klaviyo, Shopify, Slack, Notion, Google Drive, and OneDrive. All are currently
  in development.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I manage my team and monitor agent performance?">
  Use the Team section to onboard and manage your team, and the Usage page to
  get an overview of tasks accomplished by agents in your team.
</Accordion>
