Manage your team members, roles, and permissions.

## Team Members

- **Add Members**: Invite new team members via email
- **Remove Members**: Deactivate or remove team members
- **Role Assignment**: Assign appropriate roles and permissions
- **Status Management**: Track active, inactive, and pending members

## Roles & Permissions

- **Admin**: Full access to all features and settings
- **Member**: Team member
