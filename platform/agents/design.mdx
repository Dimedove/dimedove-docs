Agents can be configured to best align with your business branding and colors for a seamless brand experience.

## Key Design Features

- **Brand Alignment**: Customize colors, fonts, and visual elements to match your business branding
- **White-label Option**: Use Dimedove agents without "Powered by Dimedove" notices for complete brand consistency
- **Smart Replies**: Toggle AI-powered reply suggestions that adapt to conversation flow to optimize engagement
