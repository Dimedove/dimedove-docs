Agents can be given domain-specific expertise like Finance, Health, Legal, Engineering, and more to come.

## Configuration Options

When configuring domain expertise, businesses can:

- **Scope the Realm**: Define the specific area and depth of the domain expertise
- **Define Jurisdiction**: Specify the geographical or regulatory jurisdiction where the expertise applies
- **Custom Disclaimer**: Add disclaimers to offload accountability for information shared about the expertise
