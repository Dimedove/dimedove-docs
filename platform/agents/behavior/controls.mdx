Set boundaries to keep your agent focused, accurate, and aligned with your business goals.

## Key Controls

- **Stay On Topic**: Keep conversations focused on your main business purpose and avoid derailing
- **Hallucination Prevention**: Ground responses in facts and information you've provided
- **Define Capabilities**: Clearly specify what your agent can and cannot do for visitors
- **Content Boundaries**: Set limits on topics, actions, and information sharing
