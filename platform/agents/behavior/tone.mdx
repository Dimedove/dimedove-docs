Configure how your agent communicates with prospects and customers through tone and style preferences.

## Tone Options

- **Professional**: Formal business communication
- **Friendly**: Warm and approachable interactions
- **Casual**: Relaxed and conversational style
- **Empathetic**: Understanding and supportive responses
- **Direct**: Clear and to-the-point communication
- **Custom**: Define your own unique tone that matches your brand voice

## Tone Consistency

Ensure your agent maintains consistent tone across all interactions while adapting appropriately to different prospect and customer needs.
