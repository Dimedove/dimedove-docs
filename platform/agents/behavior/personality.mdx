Shape your agent's personality to align with your brand and create engaging customer interactions.

## Personality Traits

Configure traits that influence how your agent communicates:

- **Friendliness Level**: From professional to casual
- **Enthusiasm**: How energetic and positive responses should be
- **Empathy**: Sensitivity to customer emotions and concerns
- **Confidence**: Assertiveness in providing information and solutions
