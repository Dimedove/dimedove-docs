Webhooks allow you to receive real-time notifications and integrate Dimedove with your existing systems.

## What are Webhooks?

Webhooks are HTTP callbacks that Dimedove sends to your specified endpoints when certain events occur in your account.

## Use Cases

- **Real-time Notifications**: Get instant updates on customer interactions
- **Data Synchronization**: Keep your systems in sync with Dimedove data
- **Automated Workflows**: Trigger actions in external systems
- **Analytics Integration**: Send event data to your analytics platform
