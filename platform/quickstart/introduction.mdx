# What is Dimedove?

Dimedove is an AI-powered customer engagement platform helping businesses qualify leads and engage customers through intelligent agents. Deploy agents across multiple channels, manage all conversations from a unified inbox, and automate workflows, all while maintaining the human touch your customers value.

## Built for the AI Era

As businesses adapt to an AI-first world, customer expectations are evolving. Dimedove empowers you to create intelligent agents that provide instant, personalized interactions at scale.

## Key Capabilities

- **Intelligent Agent Builder**: Create sophisticated AI agents with custom identity, knowledge, and behavior
- **Multi-Channel Engagement**: Deploy across your website messenger, forms, email, phone, Slack, and social media
- **Lead Qualification**: Automatically identify and qualify prospects through conversations
- **Unified Inbox**: Manage all conversations across channels in one place
- **Workflow Automation**: Connect platform events to automated actions
- **Developer Tools**: REST API, webhooks, and MCP servers for custom integrations
