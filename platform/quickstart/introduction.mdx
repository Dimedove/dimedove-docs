# What is Dimedove?

Dimedove is a no-code agent building platform powering businesses selling in the AI era. We help businesses qualify leads and offer delightful customer experiences tailored for the agentic future of the web.

## Built for the AI Era

As businesses adapt to an AI-first world, customer expectations are evolving. Dimedove empowers you to create intelligent agents that provide instant, personalized interactions while maintaining the human touch your customers value.

## Key Capabilities

- **No-Code Agent Builder**: Create sophisticated AI agents without technical expertise
- **Lead Qualification**: Automatically identify and qualify potential customers
- **Customer Experience**: Deliver tailored interactions that delight your audience
- **Agentic Integration**: Seamlessly blend AI automation with human oversight
