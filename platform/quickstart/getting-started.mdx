This guide walks you through setting up your first AI agent on Dimedove.

## Prerequisites

- A Dimedove account (sign up at [dashboard.dimedove.com](https://dashboard.dimedove.com))
- Admin access to your organization

## Setup Steps

1. **Create your agent** -Set up your agent's identity, instructions, and personality to match your brand. See [Agents Overview](../agents/overview).
2. **Add knowledge** -Upload files, connect websites, or write articles to give your agent the context it needs. See [Content](../agents/content/files).
3. **Configure channels** -Deploy your agent to your website, forms, email, or other channels. See [Installation](../agents/install).
4. **Test in preview** -Use the built-in preview to test conversations before going live. See [Preview](../agents/preview).
5. **Go live** -Publish your agent and monitor conversations from the [Inbox](../inbox/overview).
