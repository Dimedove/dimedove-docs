## Overview

Dimedove Technologies Inc. ("Dimedove") acts as a data processor and engages certain subprocessors that may process personal data submitted to Dimedove's AI agent building platform and related services. These subprocessors are listed below with a description of their services, the data they may access, and data processing locations.

Dimedove services and data are hosted in Amazon Web Services (AWS) regions **US East (us-east-1)** and **Canada Central (ca-central-1)**. This list may be updated by Dimedove from time to time in accordance with the terms of our Data Processing Agreements and customer contracts.

---

## Core Subprocessors (Always Active)

These subprocessors are integral to the operation of the Dimedove platform and process data for all customers.

**Amazon Web Services, Inc.**: Cloud hosting, storage, and processing (including AI processing) of customer data. Accesses all customer data as the infrastructure provider. (USA/Canada)

**Stripe, Inc.**: Payment processing, subscription billing, and financial transaction services. Accesses billing email, payment method details, and usage meter data. (USA)

**Kinde Pty Ltd**: User authentication, identity management, and access control services. Accesses authentication credentials, user identity information, and session data. (Australia/Global)

**Resend, Inc.**: Transactional email delivery and communication services. Accesses recipient email addresses and email content for platform notifications. (USA)

**Loops Technologies, Inc.**: Email marketing and campaign delivery services. Accesses user email addresses and marketing preferences. (USA)

---

## AI Model Providers

These subprocessors provide AI inference capabilities. The specific provider(s) used depend on the Business Customer's agent configuration.

**OpenAI, L.L.C.**: AI model services and natural language processing. Accesses conversation content transmitted for AI inference. (USA)

**Google L.L.C.**: AI processing (Gemini models), analytics services, OAuth authentication, Workspace (email, calendar), and Google Drive (file storage/collaboration). Accesses conversation content for AI inference, authentication data, and connected workspace data. (USA/Global)

**Anthropic, PBC**: AI model services and processing. Accesses conversation content transmitted for AI inference. (USA)

**Groq, Inc.**: High-performance AI inference and processing services. Accesses conversation content transmitted for AI inference. (USA)

**Meta Platforms, Inc.**: AI model services and processing. Accesses conversation content transmitted for AI inference. (USA)

**ElevenLabs, Inc.**: AI voice synthesis, text-to-speech, and audio generation services. Accesses text content for speech synthesis only. Dimedove does not send voice recordings to ElevenLabs. (USA)

Customer data processing occurs primarily in:
