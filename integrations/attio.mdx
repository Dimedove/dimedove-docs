Dimedove's Attio integration connects your AI agents to Attio's flexible CRM. Automatically create and update records based on agent conversations, keeping your customer data in sync across platforms.

## Integration Features

### Supported Resources

Dimedove automatically discovers all available objects in your Attio workspace, including custom objects. Your agents can interact with any resource that your Attio account has configured.

### Agent Actions

Configure your Dimedove agents to perform CRM operations as part of their workflows:

- **Create Records**: Automatically create new records when an agent qualifies a lead
- **Update Records**: Update existing records with new information gathered during conversations
- **Upsert Records**: Look up existing records by unique fields and update them, or create new records if no match is found. Attio's native upsert support ensures reliable data consistency

### Key Capabilities

- **Dynamic Object Discovery**: Dimedove queries your Attio workspace to detect all available objects and their fields automatically
- **Unique Field Detection**: Attio's field-level uniqueness constraints are detected and used for accurate record lookups
- **Field Mapping**: Map agent conversation outputs to specific Attio fields for precise data entry
- **Unified Inbox**: Conversations that trigger CRM actions appear alongside all other channels in your Dimedove dashboard

## Getting Connected

To connect your Attio account:

1. Navigate to **Settings > Integrations** in your Dimedove dashboard
2. Click **Connect** next to Attio
3. Authorize Dimedove with your Attio account
4. Your available objects and fields will be discovered automatically
5. Configure agent actions to start syncing data

<Info>Attio integration requires a Pro subscription with third-party integrations enabled.</Info>
