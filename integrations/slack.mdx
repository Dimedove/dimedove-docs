Dimedove's Slack integration enables powerful connections between your Slack workspace and your AI agents. Connect your workspace to deploy agents directly to Slack channels and receive real-time notifications about important events.

## Integration Features

### Slack Agents

Deploy your Dimedove agents to Slack channels where team members can interact with them directly. Agents respond in threads, keeping conversations organized while providing consistent, AI-powered responses.

### Key Capabilities

- **Channel Deployment**: Add agents to public and private channels
- **Mention-Based Triggers**: Configure agents to respond when @mentioned or to all thread messages
- **Threaded Conversations**: All responses organized in threads for clean channel management
- **Unified Inbox**: All Slack conversations appear alongside other channels in your Dimedove dashboard

## Getting Connected

To connect your Slack workspace:

1. Navigate to **Settings > Integrations** in your Dimedove dashboard
2. Click **Connect** next to Slack
3. Authorize Dimedove in your Slack workspace
4. Start creating Slack Agents and deploying them to channels

<Info>Slack integration requires a Pro subscription.</Info>
