Dimedove's Pipedrive integration connects your AI agents to your Pipedrive CRM. Automatically create and update people, organizations, and deals based on agent conversations, keeping your sales pipeline current.

## Integration Features

### Supported Resources

- **People**: Create and update person records with contact details from conversations
- **Organizations**: Sync company information from qualified leads
- **Deals**: Create and manage deals in your sales pipeline

### Agent Actions

Configure your Dimedove agents to perform CRM operations as part of their workflows:

- **Create Records**: Automatically create new people, organizations, or deals when an agent qualifies a lead
- **Update Records**: Update existing records with new information gathered during conversations
- **Upsert Records**: Look up existing records by searchable fields (name, email, phone) and update them, or create new records if no match is found

### Key Capabilities

- **Dynamic Field Discovery**: Dimedove detects your Pipedrive fields automatically, including custom fields
- **Search Field Support**: Use Pipedrive's search capabilities to find and match existing records before creating duplicates
- **Field Mapping**: Map agent conversation outputs to specific Pipedrive fields for precise data entry
- **Unified Inbox**: Conversations that trigger CRM actions appear alongside all other channels in your Dimedove dashboard

## Getting Connected

To connect your Pipedrive account:

1. Navigate to **Settings > Integrations** in your Dimedove dashboard
2. Click **Connect** next to Pipedrive
3. Authorize Dimedove with your Pipedrive account
4. Your available resources (people, organizations, deals) will be discovered automatically
5. Configure agent actions to start syncing data

<Info>Pipedrive integration requires a Pro subscription with third-party integrations enabled.</Info>
