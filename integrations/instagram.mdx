Dimedove's Instagram integration connects your Instagram professional accounts to your AI agents. Automate responses to direct messages and manage comment interactions, all from your Dimedove dashboard.

## Integration Features

### Direct Message Automation

Link your Instagram professional account to a Dimedove agent that handles incoming DMs automatically. Customers who message your account receive instant, AI-powered responses without manual intervention.

### Comment Interactions

Your agents can engage with comments on your Instagram posts:

- **Public Replies**: Respond to comments directly on the post
- **Private Replies**: Send a direct message in response to a comment
- **Comment Moderation**: Hide or unhide comments as needed

### Key Capabilities

- **Automated DM Responses**: AI agents respond to incoming direct messages in real time
- **Unified Inbox**: All Instagram conversations appear alongside Slack, Messenger, and web chat in your Dimedove dashboard
- **Contact Tracking**: Instagram contacts are tracked across channels for a complete view of each customer interaction
- **Comment Management**: Monitor and respond to post comments with public or private replies

## Getting Connected

To connect your Instagram account:

1. Navigate to **Settings > Integrations** in your Dimedove dashboard
2. Click **Connect** next to Instagram
3. Authorize Dimedove with your Instagram professional account
4. Assign an agent to handle incoming messages and comments

<Info>Instagram integration requires a Pro subscription with the Social Channels feature enabled.</Info>
