Dimedove's Close integration connects your AI agents to your Close CRM. Automatically create and update leads, contacts, and opportunities based on agent conversations, keeping your sales team focused on closing deals.

## Integration Features

### Supported Resources

- **Leads**: Create and update lead records from qualified conversations
- **Contacts**: Sync contact information gathered by your agents
- **Opportunities**: Create and manage sales opportunities in your pipeline

### Agent Actions

Configure your Dimedove agents to perform CRM operations as part of their workflows:

- **Create Records**: Automatically create new leads, contacts, or opportunities when an agent qualifies a prospect
- **Update Records**: Update existing records with new information gathered during conversations
- **Upsert Records**: Look up existing records and update them, or create new records if no match is found

### Key Capabilities

- **Standard Field Schemas**: Dimedove works with Close's standard fields for each resource type, ensuring reliable data entry
- **Lookup Field Support**: Use designated lookup fields to find and match existing records
- **Field Mapping**: Map agent conversation outputs to specific Close fields for precise data entry
- **Unified Inbox**: Conversations that trigger CRM actions appear alongside all other channels in your Dimedove dashboard

## Getting Connected

To connect your Close account:

1. Navigate to **Settings > Integrations** in your Dimedove dashboard
2. Click **Connect** next to Close
3. Authorize Dimedove with your Close account
4. Your available resources (leads, contacts, opportunities) will be discovered automatically
5. Configure agent actions to start syncing data

<Info>Close integration requires a Pro subscription with third-party integrations enabled.</Info>
