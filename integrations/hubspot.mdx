Dimedove's HubSpot integration connects your AI agents directly to your HubSpot CRM. Automatically create and update contacts, companies, and deals based on agent conversations, keeping your sales team informed with real-time data synchronization.

## Integration Features

### Supported Resources

- **Contacts**: Create and update contact records with conversation data
- **Companies**: Sync company information from qualified leads
- **Deals**: Create and manage deals in your sales pipeline
- **Custom Objects**: Access and interact with custom objects defined in your HubSpot account

### Agent Actions

Configure your Dimedove agents to perform CRM operations as part of their workflows:

- **Create Records**: Automatically create new contacts, companies, or deals when an agent qualifies a lead
- **Update Records**: Update existing records with new information gathered during conversations
- **Upsert Records**: Look up existing records by unique fields (such as email) and update them, or create new records if no match is found

### Key Capabilities

- **Dynamic Schema Discovery**: Dimedove automatically detects your HubSpot fields, including custom properties, so your agents always work with the latest CRM structure
- **Field Mapping**: Map agent conversation outputs to specific HubSpot fields for precise data entry
- **Unified Inbox**: Conversations that trigger CRM actions appear alongside all other channels in your Dimedove dashboard

## Getting Connected

To connect your HubSpot account:

1. Navigate to **Settings > Integrations** in your Dimedove dashboard
2. Click **Configure** next to HubSpot
3. Authorize Dimedove with your HubSpot account
4. Your available resources (contacts, companies, deals) will be discovered automatically
5. Configure agent actions to start syncing data

<Info>HubSpot integration requires a Pro subscription with third-party integrations enabled.</Info>
