Integrate Dimedove's chatbot directly into your applications and websites.

## What is Messenger?

The Dimedove Messenger is a flexible chat interface that can be embedded in your website, mobile app, or custom application to provide seamless customer communication.

## Key Features

- **Customizable Interface**: Match your brand design and styling
- **Real-time Messaging**: Instant communication with customers
- **Mobile Responsive**: Works perfectly on all device sizes
- **Agent Integration**: Seamlessly connect with your Dimedove agents
